Space Exploration Timeline

Every step beyond our planet has reshaped our understanding of the cosmos.

1957 — Sputnik 1

The Soviet Union launches the first artificial satellite, marking humanity’s entry into space.

1961 — Yuri Gagarin

Vostok 1 carries the first human into orbit, as Gagarin becomes a symbol of human achievement.

1969 — Apollo 11

Neil Armstrong and Buzz Aldrin walk on the Moon, forever changing the course of exploration.

1998 — International Space Station

A global collaboration begins in low-Earth orbit, fostering peace and science among nations.

2012 — Curiosity Rover

NASA’s Mars rover lands in Gale Crater, uncovering evidence of ancient water and life potential.

2021 — James Webb Telescope

The most powerful telescope ever built begins unfolding the secrets of the early universe.

Legendary Astronauts

The brave pioneers who turned the impossible into human triumphs.
(Click on the astronaut's image to know more about them.)

Yuri Gagarin

First human to journey into outer space and first to orbit the Earth, cementing his status as the original "Starman" and a pivotal figure of the Space Race.

Neil Armstrong

First human to set foot on the Moon and commander of the Apollo 11 mission; also commanded Gemini 8, performing the first successful docking of two spacecraft in orbit.

Kalpana Chawla

First Indian-American woman in space and a key mission specialist on two Space Shuttle missions, STS-87 and the ill-fated scientific mission STS-107.

Chris Hadfield

First Canadian to command the ISS, first Canadian to walk in space, and the "Singing Astronaut" who popularized life in orbit through social media and the first music video recorded in space.

Sunita Williams

First woman to command the ISS, record holder for cumulative spacewalk time by a woman and a veteran of three long-duration ISS missions.

Rakesh Sharma

First Indian citizen to travel to space, where he spent nearly eight days aboard the Salyut 7 space station and conducted experiments, including remote-sensing and zero-gravity yoga.
